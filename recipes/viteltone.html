Vitel Tone

Description

Traditional christmas christmas dish from Italy, very popular in Argentina

Ingredients

  • Pecetto
  • White wine vinegar
  • leek
  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Parsley
  • Salt
  • Laurel leaves
  • Pepper corns
  • Anchovies
  • Tuna
  • Brot
  • Capers
  • Mayo
  • Mustard
  • Heavy cream

Steps

  1. Boil the meat with the vegetables and the spices
  2. For the sauce mix the anchovies, tuna and capers with some broth, then add mayo and mustard
  3. Add heavy cream to the sauce
  4. Cut the meat into thin sciles and layer with the sauce, add capers on top for decoration
